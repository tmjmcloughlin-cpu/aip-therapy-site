How to find the clinic

If you're unsure on arrival, call or message and we'll guide you in.

  1. Walk along Wilson Street from the city-centre side (St Peter's Street) and look for the red-brick frontage shown above.
  2. Keep the building on your right and continue a few metres until you reach the doorway set back slightly from the pavement.
  3. Check you're outside No. 72 — the entrance has a glass door and the Room 6 buzzer panel.
  4. Press the Room 6 buzzer so we know you've arrived and the door will release.
  5. Head up the stairs to the first landing — Room 6 is at the top.
  6. Knock on the door marked Room 6 — you're here.

Getting to the clinic

🚗

By car

Wilson Street is in Derby city centre, just off St Peter's Street. The nearest car parks are St Peter's Quarter and Bold Lane — both a 3–5 minute walk. On-street parking is available on surrounding streets with a short walk to the clinic.

🚌

By bus

Derby bus station is approximately 10 minutes on foot. Most city routes stop at or near St Peter's Street or Cornmarket, both within easy walking distance of Wilson Street. Check Trent Barton or Arriva for routes.

🚂

By train

Derby train station is approximately 15 minutes on foot, or a short taxi ride. The station is well connected to Nottingham, Birmingham, Sheffield and London St Pancras. Taxis are available directly outside the station.

🚲

By bike

Derby city centre has good cycling infrastructure. Bike parking is available on Wilson Street and surrounding streets. The clinic is within easy reach of the city's cycle routes.

♿

Accessibility

The clinic is accessed via stairs. If you have mobility concerns or require ground-floor access, please get in touch before booking and we will do our best to accommodate you, including online therapy as an alternative.

💻

Online therapy

Not able to travel to Derby? All services are available online via secure video call across the UK. Online sessions are equally effective and offer complete flexibility. Find out more about online therapy.
