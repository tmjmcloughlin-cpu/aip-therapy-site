The AIP Therapy method

A structured process built for real-life pressure, not perfect conditions.

1) Assessment & map

We identify your trigger patterns, energy bottlenecks, and highest-leverage changes for the next 30 days.

2) Targeted interventions

You learn practical regulation and cognitive tools designed for high-pressure moments.

3) Integration & consistency

We embed these tools into routines, relationships, and work rhythms so they stick.

Session structure

  • 50-minute 1:1 online sessions
  • Weekly (recommended initially) or fortnightly cadence
  • Simple between-session action plan

Typical timeframe

Most clients begin to notice change within 3–6 sessions, then move into consolidation.
