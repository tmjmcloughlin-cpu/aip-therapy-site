Conversion tracking plan

What to track so marketing decisions are based on outcomes, not guesswork.

Primary conversions

  • Call button click (tel link)
  • WhatsApp/message click
  • Contact form submitted
  • Booking link click

Secondary signals

  • Scroll depth > 60%
  • Time on page > 90 seconds
  • Clicks on service pages (trauma/PTSD/anxiety/smoking)
  • Google Maps direction clicks

Implementation notes

  1. Install GA4 on Wix.
  2. Set custom events: call_click, whatsapp_click, form_submit, booking_click, maps_click.
  3. Mark call/message/form as key events in GA4.
  4. Review weekly by landing page + service page source.

Monthly review dashboard

Track: leads by channel, lead-to-consult ratio, consult-to-client ratio, and top pages by assisted conversions.
