Integrative psychotherapy approach

Sessions combine counselling, psychotherapy, hypnotherapy and NLP tools as needed. The goal is practical progress, not endless talking without direction.

  • 50-minute sessions
  • In-person in Derby or online across the UK
  • Structured, confidential and paced to your capacity

Who this suits

Adults who are functioning on the outside but feeling overloaded internally, including professionals, parents and people recovering from prolonged stress.
