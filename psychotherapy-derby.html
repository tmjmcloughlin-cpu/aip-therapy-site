Psychotherapy in Derby

Professional psychotherapy and counselling in Derby, with online sessions available across the UK.

What I help with

  • Panic attacks and high-alert anxiety
  • Sleeplessness and recurring nightmares
  • Stress, low mood and burnout-style patterns
  • PTSD and trauma responses
  • Low confidence and self-esteem
  • Anger issues and emotional overwhelm
  • Relationship pressure and communication breakdown
  • Work stress, performance anxiety and burnout recovery
  • Emotional shutdown, overthinking and decision fatigue
  • Habits linked to stress (including avoidance patterns)

Sessions are available in Derby and online across the UK, so support is accessible wherever you are.

Integrative psychotherapy approach

Sessions combine counselling, psychotherapy, hypnotherapy and NLP tools as needed. The goal is practical progress, not endless talking without direction.

Online sessions are available across the UK, as well as in-person appointments in Derby.

  • 50-minute sessions
  • In-person in Derby or online across the UK
  • Structured, confidential and paced to your capacity

Who this suits

Adults who are functioning on the outside but feeling overloaded internally, including professionals, parents and people recovering from prolonged stress.

FAQ

Do you offer online psychotherapy sessions?

Yes. Online sessions are available across the UK via secure video call.

How long is each session?

Standard sessions are 50 minutes, with weekly or fortnightly options.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on your goals, but many clients notice meaningful changes within the first 3–6 sessions.

Can psychotherapy and hypnotherapy be combined?

Yes. Where appropriate, methods are integrated to create practical, sustainable progress.

Areas we cover

In-person psychotherapy is available in Derby, including clients from city centre, Allestree, Mickleover, Littleover, Chaddesden, Spondon and surrounding Derbyshire areas.

Online options are available across the UK, so you can access support even if you’re outside Derby.
