AEO for ecommerce (2026): product Q&A that actually ranks

Ecommerce AEO in 2026 is about removing uncertainty at the moment of purchase. Product Q&A can rank, but only if it’s structured and genuinely useful.

What to answer (prioritise these)

  • Compatibility: will it work with X?
  • Sizing/fit (with examples)
  • Shipping/returns (clear and UK-specific)
  • Care/maintenance
  • Differences between variants

Format

  • Short direct answer first
  • Then detail, then links to specs/policies
  • Use consistent headings (machines love consistency)

Done properly, Q&A reduces returns and increases conversion — and that’s the real win.
