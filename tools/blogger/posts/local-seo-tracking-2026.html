Local SEO tracking in 2026: what to measure weekly (without fancy tools)

You don’t need ten dashboards. You need a weekly habit that shows whether local visibility and enquiries are moving.

Weekly metrics (15 minutes)

  • Google Search Console: clicks + impressions (last 7 days vs previous 7)
  • Top queries: what’s rising, what’s falling
  • Top pages: which pages are winning traffic
  • GBP: calls / website clicks / direction requests
  • Leads: count + quality note (good/bad/why)

Monthly metrics (60 minutes)

  • Compare month to month in GSC (queries + pages)
  • Review landing pages and conversion rate (GA4)
  • Decide next month’s priorities (max 3)

In 2026, the advantage goes to the businesses that measure consistently, not the ones with the fanciest tool stack.
