Tracking SEO/GEO/AEO in 2026: a weekly scorecard you can actually maintain

If you’re trying to run SEO in 2026 without a scorecard, you’re basically relying on vibes. Here’s the simple system that works for owners, marketers, and SEOs.

Weekly (15–20 minutes)

  • Google Search Console: clicks + impressions (last 7 days vs previous 7)
  • Top queries: what’s rising / falling
  • Top pages: winners/losers
  • GBP (local): calls, website clicks, direction requests
  • Leads: count + quality notes

Monthly (60 minutes)

  • Query + page deltas in GSC (month vs month)
  • Landing page conversion rates (GA4)
  • 1–3 priorities for next month (max 3)
  • Content plan: 4–8 pieces that map to money pages

AEO/GEO reality check

Most tools still won’t give you perfect “AI referral” measurement. Don’t wait for perfection — track the proxies that matter: branded search growth, conversion rates, and the queries you’re being shown for.
