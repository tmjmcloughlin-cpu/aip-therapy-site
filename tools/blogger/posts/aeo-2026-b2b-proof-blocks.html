AEO for B2B service pages (2026): proof blocks that earn the click

Winning the answer is only half the job. B2B buyers still click when they need proof. Here are the proof blocks that work in 2026.

Proof block 1: outcomes (not activities)

  • Before/after metrics
  • Time-to-impact
  • Constraints (so it’s believable)

Proof block 2: process transparency

Buyers want to know what happens after “yes”. A clear process section reduces perceived risk.

Proof block 3: credible specificity

  • Who you’ve worked with (where appropriate)
  • What you specialise in
  • What you won’t do

Proof block 4: FAQs that handle objections

Answer the awkward questions: price, timelines, what’s required from the client, what happens if it doesn’t work.

Build these blocks once and you’ll convert better and give machines more confidence in your credibility.
