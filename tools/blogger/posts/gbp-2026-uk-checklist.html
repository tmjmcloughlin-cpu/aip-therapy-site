Google Business Profile in 2026: the checklist I’d use on any UK local site

GBP is still the quickest local visibility lever in the UK. Here’s the checklist I run, then maintain weekly.

Setup

  • Primary category nailed
  • Service areas realistic
  • Hours accurate (incl. holidays)
  • Website URL points to the best matching landing page

Relevance

  • Services added cleanly (no keyword stuffing)
  • Business description written like a human (service + location + differentiator)
  • FAQs answered (Q&A)

Trust

  • Review process in place
  • New photos monthly
  • Real posts (updates/offers) weekly

Weekly rhythm (15 minutes)

  • 1 post
  • Reply to reviews
  • Check performance: calls / website clicks / direction requests
