AEO in 2026: how to win ‘answer’ queries (without writing fluff)

AEO is not “write shorter”. It’s: structure your content so Google can confidently extract the answer, then give the user a reason to click for the next step.

What AEO wins look like in 2026

  • Featured snippets
  • People Also Ask
  • AI summary layers (where available)
  • How-to steps and checklists

The format that works

  • Put a 40–80 word answer block near the top
  • Use clear headings that match the question
  • Follow with examples, edge cases, and UK-specific context
  • Close with a practical CTA: what should they do next?

Stop trying to AEO everything

Use AEO where it matches intent:

  • definitions (“what is…”)
  • cost expectations
  • comparisons
  • process steps

If you want a fast AEO audit, pick your top 10 queries in Search Console and rewrite the matching sections to be answer-first.
