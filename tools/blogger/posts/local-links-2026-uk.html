Local links in 2026: safe authority building for UK businesses

Local link building in 2026 is less about “guest posts” and more about earning real mentions in places your customers already trust.

What still works

  • Local sponsorships (sports clubs, charities) with a real website mention
  • Local partnerships (suppliers, complementary services)
  • Industry memberships/directories that are legitimate
  • Local PR: actual newsworthy stories, not SEO press release farms

What to avoid

  • Paid link networks
  • Low-quality “business directories” with no traffic
  • Exact-match anchor spam

A UK-friendly link plan

  1. List 20 local orgs you genuinely engage with
  2. Offer something valuable (talk, workshop, sponsorship, resource)
  3. Ask for a mention on their site (not “a backlink”)

Authority compounds, but only if it’s real.
