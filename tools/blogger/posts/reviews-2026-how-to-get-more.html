Reviews in 2026: how to get more (without being spammy)

Reviews are a local SEO asset and a conversion asset. In 2026, the best review systems are simple, consistent, and built around great timing.

1) Ask at the moment of value

The best time to ask is when the customer has just felt the outcome (not when you’re still “doing the work”).

2) Make the action frictionless

  • One link (or QR code) that opens the review screen
  • One sentence instruction
  • No multi-step funnel

3) Don’t script the review

Instead of telling people what to say, prompt detail:

  • What problem did you have?
  • What did you like about the experience?
  • Would you recommend us, and why?

4) Reply properly

Thoughtful replies are trust signals. Keep it human, specific, and short.

5) Track it like a process

  • Asked
  • Left review
  • Follow-up needed
