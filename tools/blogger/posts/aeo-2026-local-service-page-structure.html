AEO for local service pages (2026): the answer-first page structure

If you run a local service business, your service page should be written like it’s trying to win a snippet and close the enquiry. Here’s the structure I use.

Above the fold

  • One-sentence “what we do + where”
  • Who it’s for (and who it isn’t for)
  • Primary CTA (call/book)

Answer block (40–80 words)

Give the clean answer to “What is <service>?” or “How does <service> work?”

Process steps (bullets)

  • Step 1: what happens first
  • Step 2: what happens next
  • Step 3: outcomes / next actions

Pricing expectations

Ranges are fine. Explain what changes price.

Proof blocks

  • Reviews/testimonials
  • Credentials
  • Before/after or case notes (where appropriate)

Local specifics

  • Areas served
  • Parking/access
  • Response times

This structure helps machines extract answers and helps humans decide.
