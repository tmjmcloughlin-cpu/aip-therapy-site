AEO for ecommerce category pages (2026): answer sections without thin content

Category pages are hard: you want an answer section, but you don’t want a 1,000-word essay that no one reads.

The category AEO pattern

  • 40–80 word “what is this category for?” block
  • 3–5 buyer questions answered (bullets)
  • 1 comparison: “how to choose” criteria
  • Links to deeper guides (blog) for long answers

Keep it honest

If you don’t stock a thing, say so. If shipping takes longer for bulky items, say so. In 2026, credibility beats “optimised copy”.
