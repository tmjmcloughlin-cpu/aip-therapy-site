Google Business Profile in 2026: the checklist that still works

Google Business Profile (GBP) remains the centre of gravity for local SEO. Here’s a practical checklist you can run in under an hour, then maintain weekly.

Foundation

  • Primary category: correct and specific (don’t chase keywords).
  • Secondary categories: relevant, but not overstuffed.
  • Service areas: realistic (don’t claim half the country).
  • Hours: accurate, incl. holiday hours.
  • Phone: correct and answered.
  • Website URL: goes to the best matching landing page.

Trust and relevance

  • Services: add each core service with clean names.
  • Products (if applicable): add key offers with pricing ranges.
  • Business description: plain-English, includes location and core services.
  • Photos: upload new photos monthly; remove outdated when needed.

Reviews (the compounding lever)

  • Ask after a successful outcome (timing matters more than volume).
  • Use one simple link/QR; track who asked + who left a review.
  • Reply to reviews with specifics (not copy/paste).

Weekly rhythm

  • 1 post (update, offer, FAQ)
  • Reply to new reviews
  • Check performance: calls, website clicks, direction requests
