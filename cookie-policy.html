Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device.

How we use cookies

Anything Is Possible Therapy uses cookies solely to understand how visitors use this website so we can improve it. We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or any cookies that share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies we use

Cookie / Service Provider Purpose Duration Category
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics (GA4) Counts unique visitors and tracks page views to help us understand which content is most useful. 2 years / 1 year Analytics
_clck, _clsk Microsoft Clarity Records anonymised session replays and heatmaps to help us improve page layout and usability. 1 year / session Analytics
aip_cookie_consent AIP Therapy (this site) Stores your cookie preference (accepted or declined) so the banner does not reappear on every visit. Until cleared Essential / Functional

Note: aip_cookie_consent is stored in localStorage, not as a browser cookie, but serves the same functional purpose.

Your choices

When you first visit this site, a banner gives you the option to Accept or Decline analytics cookies. If you decline, Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity will not load and no analytics cookies will be set.

You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site, which will cause the banner to reappear on your next visit.

You can also opt out of Google Analytics across all websites using Google's opt-out browser add-on, and opt out of Microsoft Clarity data collection via Microsoft's privacy settings.
