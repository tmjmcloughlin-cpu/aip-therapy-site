About James Harris

James is a clinical hypnotherapist and trained psychotherapist based in Derby. BACP-registered. In-person Derby DE1 or online across the UK.

James’ story

After a head injury brought his military career to an unexpected close, James went through a personal recovery journey that introduced him to hypnotherapy and long-term therapeutic work.

That experience shaped his mission: help clients move from survival mode to grounded confidence using practical therapy methods.

Since then, James has built businesses, led charity work, and focused his professional life on helping people recover from pressure, trauma and anxiety patterns.

How James works

Hypnotherapy-led

James leads with clinical hypnotherapy — working at the level where habits, anxiety and trauma patterns actually live. Below conscious effort, where real change happens.

Psychotherapy-informed

As a trained psychotherapist, James brings depth and structure to the work. Understanding the root of what you’re dealing with, not just managing the symptoms.

Practical and tailored

No one-size-fits-all scripts. Every session is built around where you are, what you’re dealing with, and what outcome you actually want.

FAQ

How much is a session?

Sessions are currently offered at £110 for a limited period. Exact fees are confirmed before booking.

Do you offer in-person therapy in Derby?

Yes. In-person sessions are available at Room 6, 72 Wilson St, Derby DE1 1PL.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes. Secure online sessions are available across the UK.

How long is each session?

Most appointments are 50 minutes, with frequency matched to your goals and capacity.

How quickly can I start?

Availability changes weekly. Contact us for current openings and we’ll offer the next suitable slot.

What issues can you help with?

Common areas include anxiety, panic, trauma, PTSD, stress, low mood, sleep disruption, confidence and behaviour change.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes. Sessions are confidential in line with UK professional and ethical standards, with normal safeguarding exceptions explained clearly.

Do you combine approaches?

Yes. Work can integrate psychotherapy, counselling, hypnotherapy and NLP-informed techniques where appropriate.

How many sessions will I need?

It varies by goal. Many clients report meaningful progress in the first 3–6 sessions, with longer-term support available if needed.

How do I book?

Use the contact page, call, or WhatsApp message to request a free 15-minute intro call and availability check.
